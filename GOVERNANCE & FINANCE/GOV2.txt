Title: Solutions to Improve Governance Models and Manage Innovation
 
Subject: Urban Planning

* Governance

Abstract: 

Good governance, as expressed through aspects like technical and managerial competence, reliability, accountability, transparency and citizens’ participation, is increasingly seen as a key factor to ensure sustainable prosperity. However, there is no blueprint and no single set of solutions for achieving it. This practical session will showcase innovative projects that help make good decisions to manage innovation and improve residents’ life.
