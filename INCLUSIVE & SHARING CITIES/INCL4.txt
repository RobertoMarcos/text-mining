Title: Solutions to Co-Create Inclusive and Equitable Cities

 
Subject: Collaborative Society

* Society

Abstract: 

In an inclusive and equitable city, residents have the power and resources to collectively plan, change and shape their environment. While there is no one sure-fire way to build such a city, there are successful initiatives promoting inclusive development and involving different stakeholders. This practical session will showcase a variety of solutions that encompass different key elements to co-create inclusive and equitable cities.
